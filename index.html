Feather

It's called PreMiD for a reason.

Feather is a simple, lightweight, PreMiD-compatible extension which allows you to display your web activity on your Discord profile through Rich Presence.

Feather is licensed under the MIT License and its source code is available on GitHub. You can download builds of the extension here.

Presences are available in the Feather Store. The Feather Store is built in SvelteKit and its source code is also licensed under the MIT license.

Highlights

  • Open source.
  • No ads or tracking.
  • Simple and lightweight.
  • Cleaner, organized, unrestricted RPCPlugin API.
  • Support for PreMiD presences.
  • Only asks for permissions it needs.
  • No desktop app.
